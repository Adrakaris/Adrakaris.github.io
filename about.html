Hey, so you stumbled across this website. Welcome. I started this site in October of 2020, in an effort to have something where I could put personal projects and thoughts, as well as, well, just to make a nice looking website.

Here, all the main styling is created by me from the ground up, over many hours, including the majority of art here Except those that are sourced, or are images of places. You can buy prints of the background too!

I made this site in my free time, alongside university work.

Do have an explore!
