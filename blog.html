Preface - Revision Notes

As you can see, the following are compiled, written up notes of module content taught at Warwick University. These are, in effect, lecture notes, and are made by one person, me, Yijun. These notes are provided as is.

I am not affiliated with the University of Warwick or the Department of Computer Science, however I am part of the CS Revision Guide group here at Warwick - you should go check the site out; it has much more detail on certain topics which I have skipped over.

Contributions. All contributions and corrections are welcome. If you find a mistake or something you'd like to add, I reccomend making a pull request on the repository. If you know my discord, you may also contact me there.

Plagiarism. Plagiarism is very important, and we should all take care to not violate academic integrity guidelines. All content here is from my notes, based on lectures which are available to all university students taking the relevant modules. Most figures are my own, or sourced with links. I will, however, remove access to these notes if formally notified by the Department as required.
