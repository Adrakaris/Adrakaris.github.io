Yijun Hu

Welcome to yijun.hu! I'm Yijun, a British Chinese computer science student at Warwick and a fan of worldbuilding, origami and traditional Chinese arts. Here, you'll find some of the things I have written, as well as links to other places.

Feel free to browse around, have a look at my GitHub, and the Blog.

The Blog contains notes for university modules (of course, written up using \( \LaTeX \) where required), as well as some of my origami designs, and I plan to slowly add to it as and when I can.

I also have a store for art - you can buy the background image of this site as a poster! And other things, of course.
