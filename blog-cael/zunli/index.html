寰衰

The Collapse of the Ancient Empire

Text

尊理皕十三年銀皇病而死后蔡銀愛若斯上祚銀后治其國整卅二年所治善而薄尊理皕卌年民曾大災使衆叛凡人大叛多龍因亡銀后為凡俘受刑而殺此行使多龍主甚悻尊理皕卌五年銀朝終也後戰后之兄四峽張氏之張宇靖名哈楷侖宣己爲張代始皇昕代皇帝其戰非辛使其與庶龍奔至馬氏之太穿馬主接之而應之爲實帝也張百萬歲哉反在長觀凡族武長諳宣己爲新商朝之建翌皇今太穿西域雲心張治另最土商朝之祖龍治其自凡之武家名義獻

Translation

In the Year Zunli 213, the Zunli Emperor of the Yin dynasty caught illness, and passed. His wife, Cai Yin Airos, took the throne as regent. Empress Dowager Airos ruled her country for a full 32 years. Her rule was kind, albiet weak. In Zunli 240, the people suffered a great famine, which caused great suffering and thus revolt. The common people rose up in rebellion at the ineffective rule, and many of Dragonkind were slain. The Empress Dowager was captured, tortured, and then slain also, an act that stirred deep resentment and hatred amongst the Draconic Lords towards the rebels.

In Zunli 245, the Yin Dynasty was finally extinguished. In the ensuing civil war, the Empress Dowager's brother, Zhang Yujing (given name Hakaeron) of the Four Gorges Zhang Clan (Sixia Zhang), declared himself as the Xindai Emperor of Zhang. His campaign, however, did not go successfully, and he and many dragonkind fled westwards, to the Damak Valley of Clan Ma (Taichuan Ma). Lord Ma welcomed the newly self-crowned monarch, and hence proclaimed him as the rightful ruler of the Ancient Empire, his dynasty of Zhang the true heirs to the imperial throne.

However opposing Zhang Yujing, in Changguan, was a human by the name of Wu Zhang'an, who proclaimed a new dynasty of Shang, and himself as its Jianyi Emperor.

Currently, Damak, the Western Regions, and Cloudshearth (Taichuan, Xiyu, Yunxin) are governed by the Zhang, whilst most of the other regions of the former united Empire are governed by Wu Yixian, the Zulong Emperor of Shang.
