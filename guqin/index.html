Note on ligatures and typefaces

This page uses a version of Noto Sans SC modified by me to include ligatures suitable for writing 减字谱 notation. If your browser for some reason struggles and does not like the ligatures, then this won't display properly. Annotated aligning may vary on different browsers and operating systems.

If the following is a string of jumbled letters, then your ligatures are not displaying:

\7t/da/9 -s-z7-6 \fan \1g/zh/7 \fanz \li/s-z5-4

You can find the font, termed Noto Qin, on my GitHub here.

红山

asra hakroson khrae

The numbers do not indicate pitch, but rather beat. 1 unit is equal to 1 quartet. 勾 and 挑 are not shown, and are up to the player. Play verse and chorus in the order VVC VVC.

75-80 BPM, 安宁地.
