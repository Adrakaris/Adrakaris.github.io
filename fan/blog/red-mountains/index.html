ASRA HAKROSON KHRAE

asrá hákroson khrae

Song of Red Mountains

### Latin Character In red mountains, in kingdom hall, / *Ith khansakh, ith hakaros khrae*, He walked proud and high, / *Aak dithins sirae*, Under the sun of autumn’s fall, / *Iisa asiir thera’on prin*, She danced through the sky. / *Khar su sara korins*. In red mountains, in kingdom hall, / *Ith khansakh, ith hakaros khrae*, A wise sage resides, / *Vorn dranae xirin,* Under the ground in red crystal, / *Siia dir vathe ith polor khrae,* An ancient secret lies. / *Moras xinae xirin.* *[chorus]* Oh, my child, my warrior prince, / *Akh, ron xiyor, ron khalir khras,* Seek what lies beneath! / *Zisii tha, siia hirin!* An ancient secret, when unearthed, / *Moras xinae, as ankarin,* To you, great power bequeath. / *Tao zii, xian tanie malin.* From red mountains, from kingdom hall, / *Zon khansakh, zon hakaros khrae,* With a crest of flame, / *Mer lovii yirae,* Guided below by the Red God’s claw, / *Kaethins iisa me rekhaxana,* Down the depths he came. / *Iisa vath aak komins.* From red mountains, from kingdom hall, / *Zon khansakh, zon hakaros khrae,* Down to night’s domain, / *Tao kharik xarai,* And deep below in red crystal, / *A iisa vath ith polor khrae,* He saw his love again. / *Nadins aakon ixiir xin.* *[chorus]* ### Draconic
