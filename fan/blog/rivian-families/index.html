The Noble Houses of Rivian

The current Holy Kingdom of Rivian, is a feudal semi-theocracy lead by a pope-king, who is sovereign over all provinces in the heartland, and suzerain of all dominions in the exterior. Each province is ruled by a noble family, which often change through the ages, whether by war, intrigue, or just the lack of a male heir. The current list, sorted roughly in importance within the Kingdom, shows the noble houses of the provinces, in the year 447, reign of Augustin III.

The Greater Houses

#### House of Andolyn *Sovereign of all Rivian / Duke of the Andolyn Vale* Human house with origin in Andel, Vale of Andolyn, who seized the throne in a civil war against the now extinct House of Harynton. Led by Pope Augustin III (Caesar James Augustin Andolyn), King of Rivian #### The House of Astron *Guardians of the North and Knights of the Fire Mountain* Draconic house with origin in Yirandastron Tor, Vale of Andolyn, an old house from the old Empire that has since since the time of Rivian held the Fire Mountain. Led by Lord Roarn Astron, Earl of the Fire Mountain #### The House of St. Avaryn *Lords of the Citadel and the Duchy of Majorium* Elven / half-elven house with origin in Majora, Majorium, ever the holders of the citadel and its library, given rule over the Province of Majorium after it was seized from the House Harynton. Lead by Lord Faeryn St. Avaryn, Duke of Majorium #### The House of Iridium *Lords of the Eastern Plains and the Duchy of Eridion* Elven house with origin in Ieridia, Eridion; Based in Skygate, Eridion Lead by Lord Morinyr Iridium-Farlaere, Duke of Eridion

The Marches

#### The House of Teucania *Princes of Teucania* Human house with origin in Teucanton, Lavonium; based in Leukonia, dom. of Teucania Lead by Prince Cassius Julius von Acharn, Prince of Teucania #### The House of Lower Arléyon (House of Leonparte) *Lords of the West and the Marquesses of Leonparte* Human house with origin in Arsur, march of Leonparte, with some draconic ancestry. Lead by Lady Askhan d’Arléyon, Marchioness of Leonparte #### The House of Ankaros *Marquesses of Alvycia* Human house with origin in Elaros, march of Alvcia. Lead by Lord Piotr Ankaros, Marquess of Alvycia

The Lesser Houses

#### The House of Whytport _Lords of the Duchy of Lavonium_ Human house with origin in Arkwyte, Lavonium Lead by Lord John Davenport, Duke Whytport of Lavonium #### The House of Takan-Mauen _Stewards of the Mountains and the Barony St. Makar_ Dwarven house with origin in Takan Makar, Tyres. Lead by Lord Futhark Takan-Mauen, Baron St. Makar of Takan Makar #### The House of Tyrol _The Watchers of Ironguard and the Lords of Tyr_ Human house with origin in Ironguard, Tyres, with some dwarven ancestry. Lead by Lord Jorvik Altatyr, Baron Tyr of Ironguard #### The House of Asin Khan _Lords of the Skies and the Reach of Lavonia_ Draconic house with origin in the Kin of the Mountains, Reach of Lavonia, often called the Last of the Dragons, one of two factions who stayed neutral and held on to their lands after the fall of the Old Empire. Lead by Lord Janik Asin Khan, Baron of Lavonia #### The House of Ryven _Lords of the Barony Lafayette_ Human house with origin in Ravennia, Riverlands. Lead by Lord Eldros Ryven, Baron Lafayette of the Riverlands #### The House of Caracor _Lords of the Barony de Valaros_ Tiefling house with origin in Caracor, Caracous. Lead by Lord Adriynn Caracor, Baron de Valaros of Caracous #### The House of Dufont _Chancellors of the Barony of Lancarik_ Dwarven house with origin in Lankarik, Reach of Lavonia. Lead by Chancellor Braithwite Dufont, Baroness of Lancarik #### The House of Denver _Lords of the Barony St. Gonver_ Human house with origin in Gonvorlein, Majorium Lead by Lord Girton Denver, Baron St. Gonver of Gonvorlein #### The House of Tamerlain _Lords of the Seas and the Barony Helharth-Tamyr_ Human house with origin in Tamerlain, Seawyrde Lead by Lord Albert von Tamerlain, Baron Helharth-Tamyr of Seawyrde #### The House of Kirian _Lords of the Barony de Falange_ Human house with origin in Falangia, Favium Lead by Lord Edouard Kirian, Baron de Falange of Favium #### The House of Crys _Lords of the Chancellorship of Carelyn_ Human house with origin in Carelyn, Vale of Andolyn, hailing with some elven ancestry. Lead by Chancellor Marcus von Crys of Carelyn
